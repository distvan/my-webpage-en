Running

I’ve been running since 2006. I still remember taking part in a community road race in Szeged—an amazing experience and a life-changing moment. It was challenging at first, but over time running became a regular part of my daily life. I know many people run faster than I do, but my main goal is simply to feel better. I’m happy when I see that my performance today is a little better than it was yesterday. I guess that mindset has become a habit for me.

Dogs

When I moved to my current city, Gödöllő, a childhood dream of mine came true: as an adult, I finally experienced the special bond between a human and a dog. Choosing a puppy is never easy, but after that decision, our lives changed for the better. We spent our days together, sharing fun and joyful moments. Our Collie turned out to be a perfect companion—not only at home but also for running.
